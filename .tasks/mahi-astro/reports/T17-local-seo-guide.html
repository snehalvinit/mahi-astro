Review Collection Strategy

Target: 4.5+ star average with 20+ reviews on Google within 3 months. A perfect 5.0 with zero negative reviews is flagged as suspicious by Google's AI filters.

Review Collection Workflow

1 Create a Review Link

Once GBP is set up, generate a short review link:

  • Go to your GBP dashboard > "Get more reviews"
  • Copy the review link
  • Create a short link using bit.ly or a custom domain

2 Ask at the Right Moment

  • After a successful consultation when the client expresses gratitude
  • After a prediction comes true (follow up and ask)
  • After performing a pooja or ceremony

Script: "Thank you for trusting Believe Astrology. If our guidance helped you, we'd be grateful if you could share your experience on Google. Here's a quick link: [short link]"

3 Multi-Channel Distribution

  • WhatsApp: Send review link after consultation follow-up
  • SMS: Brief message with review link 24 hours after session
  • Email: Include review CTA in follow-up emails
  • In-person: QR code in office linking to review page
  • Website: "Review us on Google" button on thank-you page

Review Response Templates

"Thank you [Name] for your kind words! We're glad our Vedic astrology guidance helped you. Wishing you cosmic blessings on your journey. - Pandit Shree Mahadev Joshi, Believe Astrology"

Constructive/Neutral Review Response

"Thank you for your feedback [Name]. We value every client's experience and continuously strive to improve. Please feel free to reach out to us directly at +91 8154992727 so we can address your concerns personally. - Believe Astrology"

Review Quality Tips

Encourage Detailed Reviews

Google's AI analyzes review content for relevance signals. Reviews that mention specific services ("Kundli reading", "career guidance", "Vastu consultation") boost your ranking for those search terms.

  • Gently suggest: "If you could mention which service helped you, it would help others find us"
  • Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)
  • Never ask for only positive reviews — authenticity matters
  • Aim for 2-3 new reviews per month for steady growth

Tracking in Google Analytics

To view UTM data in Google Analytics:

  1. Go to Reports > Acquisition > Traffic acquisition
  2. Set primary dimension to "Session source / medium"
  3. Filter by campaign to see specific platform performance
  4. Track key metrics: sessions, bounce rate, average engagement time, conversions

Set up a monthly review cadence to evaluate which platforms drive the most valuable traffic.
