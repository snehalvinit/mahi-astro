Design Direction

Theme: Hybrid Light/Dark

After analyzing 20+ astrology/spiritual websites, the most effective approach is a hybrid theme:

  • Hero sections: Dark celestial backgrounds (deep indigo/navy) with gold accents — creates mystery, cosmic feel
  • Content sections: Light cream/white backgrounds — ensures readability, feels clean and professional
  • Accent elements: Gold and saffron for CTAs, trust badges, highlights — auspicious, warm, inviting
Source: Digital Silk — "Minimalist Web Design Trends 2026" — hybrid themes outperform pure dark themes for conversion by 23%
Source: Colorlib — "Psychic Website Design 2026" — celestial hero + light content = best of both worlds

Design Principles

  1. Trust First: Credentials, experience, testimonials above the fold
  2. Instant Booking: "Book Now" visible on every screen (hero CTA, sticky header, floating WhatsApp, bottom bar)
  3. Zen Simplicity: Clean whitespace, no clutter, elegant typography
  4. Cultural Resonance: Hindu/Vedic imagery (mandalas, chakras, Om symbol) used tastefully as accents, not overwhelming
  5. Mobile First: 57-59% of traffic will be mobile; design for thumb-friendly interactions

i18n Strategy

URL Structure

LanguageCodeURL PatternExample
English (default)en/en/services/kundli-readingDefault, most content
Hindihi/hi/services/kundli-readingFor Hindi speakers
Gujaratigu/gu/services/kundli-readingPrimary Gujarat audience

Translation Architecture

Two layers of translation (matching Nilay website pattern):

  1. UI Strings: src/content/i18n/{lang}.json — nav, buttons, labels, footers, CTAs
  2. Content: src/content/services/{lang}/{slug}.json — full service/blog content per language

Language Switcher Behavior

  • URL-based switching: /en/services/vastu → /gu/services/vastu
  • Preserves current page path when switching languages
  • No cookies or client-side state needed
  • Display: EN | हिंदी | ગુજ

Fallback Strategy

  1. Try loading content in requested language
  2. Fall back to English if translation doesn't exist
  3. Error if neither language has content

Font Loading

  • Google Fonts: Playfair Display, Inter (Latin)
  • Google Fonts: Noto Sans Devanagari (Hindi)
